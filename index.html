ABOUT ME

Hello! I am currently a junior level student at Saint Louis University in the Philippines. In hopes of developing software and/or websites in the future, I am majoring in Computer Science to chase my dreams.

I grew up in Baguio City, the Summer Capital of the Philippines, and I spend most of my vacation time in San Esteban, Ilocos sur where the background of the banner above was taken. The simplicity and elegance of these places is where I got my inspiration for my style of photography and design.

I usually slack off and play video games during my free time, but in rare cases I study more about the programming languages that I know in order to improve. There are also cases when I go outside to practice my photography. I also love to look out for bugs and issues in online video games or clients that are still in their alpha or beta stage.
